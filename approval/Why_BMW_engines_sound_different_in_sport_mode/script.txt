Why does a BMW suddenly sound angrier in Sport mode? Because the car is changing more than just the badge on the dash.

In Sport mode, the throttle gets sharper, the engine holds revs longer, and the transmission shifts later. That alone makes the engine sound more aggressive.

But many BMWs also open active exhaust valves in Sport mode. That lets more sound flow out, so the exhaust note gets louder and deeper.

And on some models, the car adds a tuned engine sound through the speakers. So what you hear is a mix of real exhaust, engine behavior, and electronic enhancement.

That’s why Sport mode feels alive. It’s not just louder. It sounds ready to attack.
