Ever notice how a BMW suddenly sounds meaner in sport mode? That’s not just your imagination.

In sport mode, BMW changes how the car behaves. The throttle gets sharper, the transmission holds gears longer, and the engine revs higher before shifting. That means you hear more of the engine’s natural sound.

On many BMWs, the sound changes even more because the car plays enhanced engine noise through the speakers. So some of that aggressive growl is real, and some of it is tuned in.

The result is simple: sport mode makes the car feel faster, louder, and more alive.

BMW doesn’t just go faster in sport mode. It talks back.
