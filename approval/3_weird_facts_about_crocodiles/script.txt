Crocodiles are way stranger than you think. Here are three weird facts.

First, crocodiles can swim with almost no effort. Their tails do most of the work, and they can move so smoothly that you might not even notice one coming.

Second, they can hold their breath for a long time. Some crocodiles can stay underwater for over an hour while waiting for prey.

Third, they have built-in sun protection. Their skin is covered in thick, armored scales, and some species can even absorb heat through special spots on their bodies.

So yeah, crocodiles are basically ancient, silent, armored ambush machines.
